Smart Indoor Farming to reduce CO2 emission

As per the latest CO2 emission report of Ireland, there is a need to provide attention to such rapidly increasing CO2 level and the Irish government is aiming to contribute to reducing this worldwide carbon emission issue. CarboAgri wants to participate in this mission by developing indoor farming in this challenging climatic country for vegan production. Attached control units in this setup take care of managing required healthy farming environmental conditions and notify the status on AgriCarbo farm application. Along with vegan production in Ireland weather, this plant indirectly provides the solution to ever growing CO2 emission.

Main Attractions

(Unique Value Proposition)

Automated climate control

Ireland's climate is not suitable for vegan farming. Irrespective of weather condition control units automatically detect and maintain environment based on the type of crop

Warnings and Alerts with real-time monitoring

Farms become smart enough to detect and notify the required time period of growth and alert if manual attention is necessary.

Four-season production

Production is independent of weather condition hence the removal of seasonal production barrier.

Pesticide Free

The environment is naturally created and hence explicit pesticide supply is not required.

Cloud Connected

IoT sensors enable remote monitoring and management of farms to reduce human error and increase farm productivity.

Fresh Natural Food With Smart Technology

Environment control and in-country vegetables and fruits production results in fresh food.

Land Free

Farming is inside the building that reduces need of open free space.

Vegan farming in Ireland

Technology provides a solution to vegetarians food problem inside the country only.

Low cost food

Home country farming provides import duty-free low-cost farm fresh food.

top competitors

Alesca

Alesca is a top competitor in indoor farming established in Beijing, China in 2013. Alesca provides compact container cabinet commercial scale automated monitoring and food production.

Plenty

Plenty aims to develop vertical plant sciences for crops to flourish in a pesticide- and GMO-free environment. Plenty founded in 2014 in San Francisco.

AeroFarms

AeroFarms is seen as a 2nd top competitor in indoor farming founded in Newark in 2004. AeroFarms provides the farming solution for faster harvest cycles without sun and soil.

BrightFarms

BrightFarms is a competitor founded in 2010 in Irvington, New York. It competes AgriCarbo in terms of providing better, healthier and environmentally friendly production.


AgriCarbo is the only player in Ireland providing all in one solution to grow farm fresh healthier food for Ireland's vegans. Irrespective of weather conditions IoT controllers create required environment to grow indoor crops that taste good, and is better for the environment and contribute to resolving widely progressing CO2 emission problem.
